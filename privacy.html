Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2025

MindGlow ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy describes how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the MindGlow application.

Information We Collect

  • Account information: If you create an account, we may store your email address and profile details you provide.
  • Usage data: We may collect information about how you use the app (e.g., feature usage, session length) to improve the product.
  • Device information: We may collect device type, operating system, and similar technical data necessary for the app to function correctly.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the MindGlow app and your experience.
  • To communicate with you about the app, updates, or support.
  • To comply with applicable laws and protect our rights.

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

Data Storage and Security

We use industry-standard practices to store and protect your data. Data may be processed and stored using services such as Firebase or other providers in accordance with their privacy policies.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your personal data. Contact us using the information below to exercise these rights.

Third-Party Services

The app may use third-party services (e.g., analytics, authentication, cloud storage). Their use of your information is governed by their respective privacy policies.

Children's Privacy

MindGlow is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the app or via email where appropriate.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your data, please open an issue or discussion in the MindGlow repository or contact the maintainers through the repository.
