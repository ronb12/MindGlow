## 🔧 **WHAT WAS FIXED**

### **Problem:**
- ❌ Background colors not visible
- ❌ Overlay was 95% opaque (hiding colors)
- ❌ Applied to wrong element

### **Solution:**
- ✅ Reduced overlay to 85% opacity
- ✅ Apply background directly to `<body>`
- ✅ Content overlay lighter for readability
- ✅ Colors now clearly visible

### **Code Changes:**
```javascript
// Before (95% opaque - colors hidden)
mainContent.style.background = `
  linear-gradient(rgba(255,255,255,0.95), rgba(255,255,255,0.95)), 
  ${bgColor}
`;

// After (85% opacity - colors visible!)
body.style.background = bgColor;
mainContent.style.backgroundColor = 'rgba(255, 255, 255, 0.85)';
```
